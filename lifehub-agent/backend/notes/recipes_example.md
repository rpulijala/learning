# Recipe Collection

## Healthy Breakfast Smoothie

A quick and nutritious breakfast option.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 banana
- 1 cup spinach
- 1/2 cup frozen berries
- 1 tablespoon almond butter
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1 scoop protein powder (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Add all ingredients to blender
2. Blend until smooth
3. Pour and enjoy!

## Quick Chicken Stir Fry

Great for meal prep and busy weeknights.

**Ingredients:**
- 1 lb chicken breast, cubed
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas)
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

**Instructions:**
1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan or wok
2. Cook chicken until browned (5-7 minutes)
3. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute
4. Add vegetables and stir fry 3-4 minutes
5. Add soy sauce and toss to combine
6. Serve over rice or quinoa

## Overnight Oats

Perfect for busy mornings - prep the night before.

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup milk of choice
- 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 1 tablespoon honey
- Fresh fruit for topping

**Instructions:**
1. Combine oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, and honey in a jar
2. Stir well and refrigerate overnight
3. Top with fresh fruit in the morning
